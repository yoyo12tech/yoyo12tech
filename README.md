## Publications
My undergraduate research on Alzheimer’s disease prediction—comparing traditional machine learning models with ensemble approaches on the OASIS-2 dataset—has been published in Nature Scientific Reports.[![Nature](https://img.shields.io/badge/Published%20in%20Nature%20Scientific%20Reports-1D9E75?style=flat-square&logo=pubmed&logoColor=white)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-026-37122-9)

The work presents a structured ML pipeline for early detection, benchmarking classical models (Logistic Regression, SVM, Decision Trees) against ensemble methods. Results show that ensemble approaches achieve superior accuracy and generalization, reaching up to 95% accuracy, while emphasizing robustness, stability, and reproducibility for real-world clinical applicability.
